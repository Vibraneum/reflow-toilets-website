## CTA Button Design Guidelines

### Visual Hierarchy

**Primary CTA:**
- Large, prominent buttons
- High-contrast colors (e.g., gold/black, blue/white)
- Bold text
- Above the fold placement

**Secondary CTA:**
- Medium-sized buttons or text links
- Complementary colors
- Standard weight text
- Mid-page placement

**Tertiary CTA:**
- Text links or small buttons
- Subtle styling
- Footer or sidebar placement

### Color Recommendations for Enterprise

**Trust & Authority:**
- Deep blue + white text
- Dark gray + gold accent
- Navy + light blue

**Action & Urgency:**
- Green (environmentally aligned)
- Gold/yellow (premium positioning)
- Red (limited spots/urgent)

**Avoid:**
- Bright, playful colors
- Multiple competing CTA colors on same page
- Low-contrast combinations

## CTA Copy Formula

### Structure: [Action Verb] + [Specific Outcome] + [Credential/Qualifier]

**Examples:**

✅ GOOD:
- "Request Implementation Proposal — Gates Foundation Technology Partner"
- "Schedule Site Assessment — 270+ Deployments Proven"
- "Download Technical Specifications — ISO30500 Certified"

❌ BAD (Too Vague):
- "Learn More"
- "Get Started"
- "Click Here"
- "Find Out More"

## CTA Placement Best Practices

### Homepage
- **Above fold:** Primary CTA (Request Implementation Proposal)
- **After Key Features:** Secondary CTA (Download Specifications)
- **Main CTA Section:** Primary + Secondary options
- **Footer:** Low-commitment CTA (Subscribe to Updates)

### Product/Solution Pages
- **Top of page:** Context-specific primary CTA
- **After each major section:** Related secondary CTAs
- **End of page:** Main conversion CTA

### Content Pages (Blog, Case Studies)
- **Header:** Information-gathering CTA (Download Case Study)
- **Sidebar:** Sticky secondary CTA
- **End of article:** Conversion CTA based on topic

## Urgency & Scarcity Tactics (Use Sparingly)

**Appropriate for Enterprise:**
- "Q2 2025 Implementation Slots Available"
- "Limited Pilot Program Capacity"
- "Reserve Your Deployment Timeline"
- "Early Adopter Pricing Available"

**Avoid (Too Consumer-Focused):**
- "Act Now!"
- "Limited Time Offer!"
- "Don't Miss Out!"
- "Hurry, While Supplies Last!"
