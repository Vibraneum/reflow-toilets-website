## Technical Specifications Summary

### b-CRT (Public Toilet Product)
- **Full Name**: blackwater-Community Reinvented Toilet
- **Capacity**: 200 flushes/day
- **Types**: Container integrated (mobile) | Buried underground split (fixed)
- **Standards**: ISO30500-2018 ✓, GB/T18920-2020 ✓
- **Locations**: Beijing (Fangshan, Xicheng, Dongcheng), Jiangsu (Yixing)

### b-HRT (Household Toilet Product)
- **Full Name**: blackwater-Household Reinvented Toilet
- **Capacity**: 100L/day (blackwater), 200L/day (greywater)
- **Dimensions**: 1500mm L × 1050mm W (single module)
- **Scalability**: Multi-module combinations available
- **Standards**: ISO30500-2018 ✓, GB/T18920-2020 ✓
- **Features**: Convenient to move, quick assembly, beautiful and durable, flexible selection

### Treatment Process (Both)
1. Toilet blackwater input
2. Biological purification tank (AOAO: Anaerobic-Aerobic-Anaerobic-Aerobic)
3. Advanced treatment tank (phosphorus, odor, color removal)
4. Disinfection (UV or ECR)
5. Reuse or compliant discharge

### Optional: Sludge Harmless Treatment
- Electrocatalysis (kills E. coli, harmful bacteria)
- Thermal treatment (destroys parasite eggs)
- Output: Safe for gardens, farmland, or disposal
