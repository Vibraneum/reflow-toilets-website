### Tech Page Updates

#### Add Section: "AOAO Biological Process"
**Anaerobic Phase**: Large molecules decompose into easily biodegradable small molecules

**Aerobic Phase**: Microorganisms continuously adsorb and oxidize organic matter, reducing ammonia nitrogen and COD

**Result**: Natural, efficient pollutant reduction

#### Add Section: "Advanced Treatment Technology"
**Filtration**: Advanced filler materials degrade organics and filter suspended solids

**Multi-Function**:
- Phosphorus removal
- Deodorization
- Decolorization
- SS reduction

#### Add Section: "Intelligent Disinfection"
**UV Disinfection** (Standard Applications):
- Destroys DNA of bacteria, viruses, fungi
- Prevents reproduction and growth
- Chemical-free sterilization

**ECR Disinfection** (High Microbial Loads up to 10^10 cfu/mL):
- Produces strong oxidants (·OH, HOCl/ClO-)
- Converts organics to CO2
- Converts ammonia nitrogen to N2
- Kills all bacteria and viruses

**Smart Selection**: System automatically chooses appropriate method based on microbial load

Our bio-mimetic approach uses natural processes—the same anaerobic and aerobic cycles found in wetlands and soil—to purify water without harsh chemicals or energy-intensive methods.

### FAQ Section Additions

**Q: What makes ReFlow/Eco-san technology different from other toilet systems?**
A: Our technology won first prize in the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation's global "Toilet Challenge" and we are the world's first company certified under ISO30500 by TUV SUD. Our AOAO biological process mimics nature's own purification methods.

**Q: What standards does the treated water meet?**
A: Our effluent meets both international (ISO30500-2018) and Chinese national (GB/T18920-2020) standards for water quality and reuse.

**Q: How much water can your systems handle?**
A: b-CRT (public): 200 flushes/day. b-HRT (household): 100L/day for blackwater, 200L/day for greywater. Multi-module configurations available for larger capacities.

**Q: What happens to the sludge?**
A: We offer an optional sludge harmless treatment system that uses electrocatalysis and thermal treatment to create completely safe output that can be used in gardens or farmland.

**Q: Can I monitor the system remotely?**
A: Yes, our IoT management platform provides multi-terminal access with fault detection, early warning, visual displays, and operational efficiency tracking.

**Q: What disinfection method do you use?**
A: We offer two options: UV disinfection for standard applications, and ECR (electrochemical) disinfection for high microbial loads. The system can intelligently select based on conditions.

**Q: Is this technology proven in real-world applications?**
A: Yes, we have successful installations in Beijing (Fangshan, Xicheng, Dongcheng) and Jiangsu (Yixing), with documented performance data meeting all standards.

**Q: How is this different from traditional sewage treatment?**
A: Our "returning to nature with the power of nature" philosophy uses biological processes instead of harsh chemicals. The modular design requires no sewage connection, has low energy consumption, and simple maintenance.
