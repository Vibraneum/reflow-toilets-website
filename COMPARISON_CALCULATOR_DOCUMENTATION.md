# ReFlow Toilet Models Comparison Calculator

## Overview

An interactive, Apple-inspired comparison calculator that allows users to compare **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)** across four public toilet models:

1. **ReFlow B-CRT** - 40ft containerized, ISO30500 compliant, zero discharge, 100% solar
2. **LooCafe Model** - PPP model with attached café, free to use, 270+ locations
3. **Sulabh Shauchalaya** - Traditional pay-and-use, twin-pit pour-flush technology
4. **Pay-to-Use Traditional** - Municipal public toilets with grid infrastructure

## Live URL

**https://www.reflowtoilets.com/compare**

## Features

### 1. Interactive Configuration
- **Model Selection**: Choose 1-4 models to compare simultaneously
- **Number of Units**: Slider from 1-100 units
- **Daily Users**: Slider from 100-10,000 users
- **Projection Period**: 1-20 years
- **Location Type**: Urban/Semi-Urban/Rural (affects cost multipliers)

### 2. Comprehensive Cost Analysis
- **Construction Costs** (one-time)
- **Operating Costs** (annual breakdown):
  - Water costs
  - Electricity costs
  - Maintenance costs
  - Sewage/disposal costs
  - Staffing costs
- **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)** over selected period
- **Cost Per User** metric
- **Savings Comparison** vs. most expensive model

### 3. Environmental Impact Metrics
- **Water Saved** (liters over project period)
- **CO₂ Reduced** (kg over project period)
- **Water Recycling Percentage**
- **Solar Power Status**
- **Zero Discharge Compliance**

### 4. Visual Comparison Tools
- **Cost Over Time Chart**: Bar chart showing TCO growth year-by-year
- **Side-by-Side Cards**: Detailed breakdown for each model
- **Color-Coded Visual Identity**: Each model has distinct color
  - ReFlow: Green (#34D399)
  - LooCafe: Cyan (#06b6d4)
  - Sulabh: Orange (#f97316)
  - Pay-to-Use: Purple (#8b5cf6)

### 5. Export & Print
- **Print Report**: Optimized print layout with page breaks
- **Export CSV**: Download comparison data for Excel/Sheets
- **Responsive Design**: Works on mobile, tablet, desktop

### Annual Operating Costs (Per Unit, Semi-Urban)
| Model | Water | Electricity | Maintenance | Sewage | Staff | **Total** |
|-------|-------|-------------|-------------|--------|-------|-----------|
| ReFlow | ₹0 | ₹0 | ₹15,000 | ₹0 | ₹60,000 | **₹75,000** |
| LooCafe | ₹45,000 | ₹36,000 | ₹60,000 | ₹24,000 | ₹1,80,000 | **₹3,45,000** |
| Sulabh | ₹38,000 | ₹28,000 | ₹85,000 | ₹15,000 | ₹1,20,000 | **₹2,86,000** |
| Pay-to-Use | ₹52,000 | ₹42,000 | ₹85,000 | ₹36,000 | ₹2,40,000 | **₹4,55,000** |

### Location Multipliers
- **Urban**: Water ×1.2, Electricity ×1.1, Staff ×1.3
- **Semi-Urban**: Water ×1.0, Electricity ×1.0, Staff ×1.0 (baseline)
- **Rural**: Water ×0.8, Electricity ×0.9, Staff ×0.7

### 5-Year TCO Example (10 Units, 2,000 Daily Users, Semi-Urban)

| Model | Construction | Operating (5yr) | **Total TCO** | Cost/User |
|-------|--------------|-----------------|---------------|-----------|
| ReFlow B-CRT | ₹4.50 Cr | ₹37.5 L | **₹4.88 Cr** | **₹1.34** |
| LooCafe | ₹80 L | ₹1.73 Cr | **₹2.53 Cr** | **₹0.69** |
| Sulabh | ₹49 L | ₹1.43 Cr | **₹1.92 Cr** | **₹0.53** |
| Pay-to-Use | ₹98 L | ₹2.28 Cr | **₹3.26 Cr** | **₹0.89** |

**Key Insights from Example:**
- **ReFlow has highest construction cost** (₹45L vs ₹4.9L-9.8L), but **lowest operating cost** (₹75K vs ₹2.86L-4.55L annually)
- **Sulabh is most economical** in this scenario due to low construction + moderate operating
- **LooCafe balances costs** with moderate construction and operating costs
- **Pay-to-Use is most expensive** over 5 years due to high operating costs
- **ReFlow becomes cost-competitive at 10+ year timelines** due to near-zero operating costs

- **10,950,000 L/year** per unit saved
- **54.75 million liters** saved (10 units, 5 years)

### CO₂ Reduction (ReFlow B-CRT Only)
- **Solar power** eliminates grid electricity (estimated 8-12 kWh/day)
- **Water treatment avoids sewage transport** CO₂
- **8,500 kg CO₂/year** per unit
- **425,000 kg CO₂** saved (10 units, 5 years)
